Frequently Asked Questions

How often should the litter be changed?

We recommend full cleanings every 1-2 weeks for single-cat households, and 2-3 times weekly for multi-cat homes. Our specialists will advise during your consultation.

Do you provide the litter?

Yes! All plans include premium litter. We offer clay, silica, pine, and organic options. Let us know your cat's preference.

What if my cat is shy with strangers?

Our team is trained in feline behavior. We'll work around your cat's comfort level, often scheduling visits when they're most relaxed.

Is there a contract?

No long-term commitments. Cancel anytime with 48 hours notice. Monthly subscribers get 10% off.
